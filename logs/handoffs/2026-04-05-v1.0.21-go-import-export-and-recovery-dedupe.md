# Handoff Archive — v1.0.21 Go Import/Export and Recovery Dedupe

## Summary
This pass ported Go-native import/export support for the settings portability workflow and refined failed-session recovery dedupe to reduce redundant recovery guidance.

## Shipped
- Added `GET /api/export` to the Go API.
- Added `POST /api/import` to the Go API.
- Export/import now covers Keeper settings, templates, debates, and repo-path mappings.
- Refined failed-session recovery by detecting recent existing recovery guidance before sending another recovery message.
- Bumped version surfaces to `1.0.21`.

## Why This Matters
This removes another practical Bun-only settings workflow and improves the quality of Go-side recovery behavior under repeated failure-state polling.
